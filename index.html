Alumni

All Teacher

My journey with NCS, Cbe began in March, 2016. It has been a wonderful tenure with the school. I felt more momentous in NCS, for the first time after years when I stood in a classroom and realized that being a teacher makes my life more meaningful. The school has given me a good exposure and extended the margins into the field of education. Every day at NCS has something to give, something to gain, something to learn and advance. . It is worthy to mention that the staff is being cared for in every way, personally and professionally at NCS and the management never fails to keeps us motivated. As a team we were always on the ball, encouraging and supporting each other in a task big or small. To have begun as the class teacher for class 1, it was a head start for my teaching career. Being with children, growing them up, teaching and learning along with them certainly makes a difference. I understood that teaching requires no arcane knowledge but only the love which every little heart needs. I must say that it is the children who keep my spirits high and every little acknowledgement that they make are my most cherished rewards. I have made many fond memories and have had remarkable experiences which will never fade. I feel very proud and contented to be a part of this prestigious institution and looking forward for many more years of association with NCS.
